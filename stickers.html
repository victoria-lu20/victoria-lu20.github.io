Turning mindless doodles into stickers

Overview: I created these designs during my free time; after creating them, I turned them into stickers, which I've sold at sticker sales for my high school Make-A-Wish club and on RedBubble. One of my favorite mediums for drawing is ink and paper, and sometimes, just mindlessly doodling can create surprising designs. The fish design on the right is by far one of my favorite pieces that I have done in the past.
